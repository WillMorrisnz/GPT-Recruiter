About Selfwealth:

Selfwealth is a rapidly growing FinTech scale-up revolutionizing the Australian investing landscape. We empower everyday Australians to take control of their financial future with a low-cost, user-friendly investment platform. Our mission is to make investing accessible and affordable for everyone.

About the Role:

We're seeking a passionate and skilled AWS Engineer to join our talented team and play a key role in building and maintaining our robust, scalable cloud infrastructure. You'll be a crucial part of ensuring our platform's high performance, security, and reliability, allowing us to deliver an exceptional experience for our growing user base.

Responsibilities:

Design, implement, and manage secure and scalable cloud infrastructure solutions on AWS, utilizing services like EC2, S3, SQS, DynamoDB, Lambda, ECS, and CloudFormation.
Automate infrastructure deployments and provisioning through IaC tools like Terraform and Github Actions.
Develop and implement CI/CD pipelines for containerized applications using ECS and Fargate.
Implement comprehensive monitoring and alerting solutions to ensure platform health and proactively address potential issues.
Perform ongoing performance optimization and cost management of the cloud infrastructure.
Collaborate with developers and other engineers to integrate AWS services into applications and microservices.
Stay up-to-date with the latest AWS technologies and best practices, and actively contribute to our internal knowledge sharing processes.
Participate in security reviews and ensure adherence to strict security standards.
Qualifications:

3+ years of experience working as an AWS Engineer or in a similar cloud-based role.
Proven experience designing, building, and managing secure and scalable cloud architectures.
In-depth knowledge of AWS services, including EC2, S3, SQS, DynamoDB, Lambda, ECS, CloudFormation, IAM, and AWS CLI.
Strong understanding of CI/CD principles and experience with tools like Terraform and Github Actions.
Experience with containerization technologies like Docker and Kubernetes.
Excellent problem-solving skills and a proactive approach to identifying and resolving technical issues.
Strong communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to work effectively with cross-functional teams.
Passion for FinTech and enthusiasm for contributing to Selfwealth's mission to democratize investing.
Bonus Points:

Experience with serverless architectures and event-driven programming.
Experience with AWS Step Functions and AWS X-Ray.
AWS Certifications (e.g., Solutions Architect Associate, Developer Associate).
